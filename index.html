Darasa FAQ

Darasa Requirements?


You should be online.
Location should be on.

First time using darasa lecture app?


Well you will have to create an account first. If you already have an account proceed by clicking on the menu at top left of your screen then click manage your password under password to set up your password.

How to add classes?


Scroll up and click on add a class under manage your classes.
Click the button continue and follow the dialogue of 3 simple steps. After all steps ensure your have keyed in the unit name, unitcode, you have selected the time of the lecture, the class venue, department and the course. In the last bit ensure you have named the semester, year of study, school and the academic year. Having filled all the details you can click the finish button. In case of success in the class creation you will receive a message to acknowledge that.

How to record a class?


Click generate when the class happens for the students to record the attendance.
In case of a student who does not own a smartphone simply click the menu at the left and click add attendee key in your password and a list of all the students will pop up select the student you wish to record his or her attendance and click ok.

How to terminate class?


To terminated the class click the menu at the left and click add attendee key in your password and you should receive a message that the class was recorded.
